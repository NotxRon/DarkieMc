    OUR HISTORY

    DarkieMc has been around for over 2 years now. What started off as a small community Minecraft server has developed into something we couldn't even imagine.

    OUR STAFF

    The MyServer staff team are friendly, dedicated, and always online to help everything run smoothly. Have any questions? Make sure to do /staff ingame to reach out!

    OUR SERVERS

    We have Survival, LifeSteal, and KitPvp on our network, all with fully custom builds and useful perks and features. Join today at play.myserver.com!

    OUR GAMES

    SURVIVAL

    Survival mode is a classic Minecraft gamemode where you start with nothing and must gather resources to survive. Face off against dangerous mobs or team up with friends to thrive in the wild! You'll encounter tough challenges as you explore, mine, and build your way through the world, earning rewards for your hard work. It takes persistence, strategy, and determination to rise above and master the world around you!

    LIFESTEAL

    Lifesteal is an intense, PvP-focused gamemode where every heart matters. When you defeat other players, you steal their hearts, making yourself stronger and them weaker. Work with friends to build your base, defend it, and conquer others. We offer exciting features like custom enchantments and a convenient shop GUI. Battle against other players and aim to become the strongest, where survival isn't just about skillâ€”it's about who can steal the most life!

    KITPVP

    KitPvP is a competitive, fast-paced gamemode packed with players! Choose your kit and join the race to the top of the leaderboard. Explore our custom map and test your skills on parkour in secret rooms. Whether you are an archer, gladiator or tactician there is a kit for you. Join the fight today on MyServer!
